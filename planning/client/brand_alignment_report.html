Section 03

Homepage Section Flow

Reordering based on cross-site consensus: establish offer before visual proof, convert before validating.

Current
Hero → Gallery → Services → Blog → Testimonials → CTA
Recommended
Hero → Services → Gallery → CTA → Testimonials

Section 06

Conflicts & Gaps

Critical issues vs. moderate opportunities vs. already-aligned elements.

🔴 Critical
White surface (#FFFFFF)
All 3 inspo sites and 0 of 5 competitors use white. Change COLOR_SURFACE to #0D0907 — single biggest visual mismatch.
🔴 Critical
Blog in HOME_SECTIONS
Zero of 8 analyzed sites (3 inspo + 5 competitors) surface a blog on the homepage. Remove it from HOME_SECTIONS.
🔴 Critical
PERSONALITY: "bold" too generic
Replace with "editorial-dark indulgent playful" — this directive is actionable and specific, "bold" is not.
🟡 Moderate
No branded loading screen
2 of 3 inspo sites use a loading sequence. NeonPulse logo + fade reveal would immediately elevate the editorial feel.
🟡 Moderate
No origin story callout
All 5 competitors use a founding story as a trust anchor. Embed a callout card inside the gallery section.
🟡 Moderate
Tacoma identity absent from SEO fields
Zero competitors target Tacoma — it's an open keyword. Add to SEO_TITLE and SEO_DESCRIPTION.
🟢 Aligned
HERO_VARIANT, NAV_STYLE, FONT_PRIMARY, FONT_SECONDARY, COLOR_ACCENT
All confirmed against inspo and competitor data. No changes needed.
🟢 Aligned
BRAND_THREE_WORDS + BRAND_CELEBRITY
"Bold Sensory Playful" and "Wes Anderson" both confirmed against inspo site signals. High confidence.
